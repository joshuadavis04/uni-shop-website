About us

Welcome to the Luton Town Marketplace! Your one-stop shop for all things Luton memorabilia related. All products listed on our site have been ensured for quality and authenticity, by our in-house experts. Our mission is to provide a great service to the supporters of the best club in the world. Due to the nature of the products being second-hand, the ecological footprint is limited compared to buying new. This is because buying pre-loved goods reduces the demand for new products, thus reducing the potential carbon footprint, and saving products from reaching landfill.
